$$ A = \text{"the number of heads is odd"} $$
and
		$$ B = \text{"the number of tails is even"} $$

a) $\Pr(A) = \Pr(B)$

b) $\Pr(A) < \Pr(B)$

c) $\Pr(A) > \Pr(B)$
