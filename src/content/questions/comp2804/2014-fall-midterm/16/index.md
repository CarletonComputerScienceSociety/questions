Consider 10 boxes and 10 balls. We throw each ball, uniformly, in a random box. What is the probability that, after we have thrown all 10 balls, none of the 10 boxes is empty?

a) $\frac{10!}{10^{10}}$

b) $\frac{10^{10}}{10!}$

c) $1 - \frac{10 \cdot (9/10)^{10}}{10^{10}}$

d) None of the above.
